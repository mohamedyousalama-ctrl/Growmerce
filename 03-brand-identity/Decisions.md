# Decisions — Brand Identity

Locked decisions for this workstream. Build on these; do not re-litigate. Changes require a new
decision entry.

---

### D-03.1 — Identity expresses the company that already exists
Brand identity translates the already-defined Growmerce — its intelligence (20), experience (04),
and voice (21) — into visual form. It does not invent the brand; it gives it a face. Designing
identity *after* strategy is deliberate, so the visuals express a real company, not a guess.

### D-03.2 — This workstream is a strategic brief, not the design
No frontend code, no final logo files, no visual mockups. It prepares the brief Claude Design uses;
it does not replace Claude Design. It defines principles, constraints, metaphors, and requirements —
not final fonts or hex values.

### D-03.3 — The identity must express the real positioning
Online Sales Growth Operations, powered by Commerce Intelligence. Never look like a marketing
agency, generic consultancy, SaaS dashboard, content company, or shallow AI tool. The north-star
feeling: "this company understands online sales deeply," not "this is a nice-looking agency."

### D-03.4 — Selected direction: Growmerce Commerce Intelligence OS
Direction A — Commerce Intelligence, evolved into a calm, premium, Arabic-first commerce
intelligence operating system / command center. Inspiration (Spotlight, Raycast, Linear, Bloomberg
Intelligence, Arc, Notion AI) is reference only — copy none. (`Visual_Direction_Principles.md`.)

### D-03.5 — The identity must feel: smart, premium, calm, operational, evidence-based, modern, Arabic-first, practical, high-trust
And must avoid: agency aesthetics, neon AI gradients, generic startup visuals, robot imagery, brain
icons, fake futuristic interfaces, crypto/Web3, dark-dashboard overload, corporate consulting
visuals. (`Brand_Identity_Strategy.md`.)

### D-03.6 — Calm intelligence (the core aesthetic resolution)
Sophistication is shown through structure, precision, evidence, and restraint — never through
density, darkness, or noise. Growmerce borrows Bloomberg's seriousness rendered with Spotlight/
Linear calm. Light-primary, not a dark dashboard.

### D-03.7 — Arabic-first is a strategic stance, not localisation
The Arabic name (جرومرس), typography, and layout are primary and premium; Latin/English integrate
within an RTL-native system. No English-first design with Arabic bolted on. The Arabic wordmark is
a crafted asset. (`Arabic_Name_and_Naming_System.md`, `Typography_Direction.md`.)

### D-03.8 — Personality maps directly to visual principles
Each narrative-personality trait (21) translates to a concrete visual principle: evidence-based →
show provenance; operational → show flows/priorities; calm → remove noise; honest → show
unknowns/ruled-out; etc. (`Brand_Personality_to_Visual_Principles.md`.)

### D-03.9 — Logo: explore concepts, avoid clichés; bilingual wordmark is central
Explore intelligence-signal / commerce-flow / pattern-recognition / OS-core / structured-network /
decision-mark / bilingual-lockup. Never: growth arrow, shopping cart, generic G, AI brain, robot,
agency icon, ecommerce bag. The bilingual wordmark is likely the heart of the identity.
(`Logo_Direction_Brief.md`.)

### D-03.10 — Typography is Arabic-first and load-bearing
The Arabic typeface decision leads; Latin pairs to it. In this restrained direction, type and space
carry the brand. Explore modern-geometric / premium-editorial / operator-intelligence-hybrid;
likely a display + functional two-tier system. (`Typography_Direction.md`.)

### D-03.11 — Colour is a semantic system, not decoration
Role-based palette (base, surface, text, brand, growth, profit, confidence/evidence, action/
priority, risk, neutral data). Profit and revenue are visually distinct. A calm confidence scale
without red-amber-green alarmism. Avoid neon, cheap green, loud red, generic SaaS blue, crypto
gradients, dark-only. No final hex here. (`Color_Direction.md`.)

### D-03.12 — Iconography is systems, signals, and flows — never AI clichés
Use system diagrams, signal maps, commerce flows, confidence markers, pattern nodes, channel/
ecosystem maps. Avoid generic AI icons, robots, brains, dashboards, carts, random 3D, stock photos.
Illustration = intelligent diagramming. (`Iconography_and_Illustration_Principles.md`.)

### D-03.13 — Motion is subtle, purposeful, and state-driven
Motion communicates state and reasoning (search, selection, accept/reject/edit, diagnosis progress,
evidence reveal, confidence change, priority) — never decoration or AI-magic theatre. Confidence-
change and honest reasoning-progress are signature motions. (`Motion_and_Interaction_Principles.md`.)

### D-03.14 — The intelligence is made visible as a system
The Brain (20) gets a visual metaphor — a calm cognition pipeline / OS core with pattern nodes,
evidence tiers, confidence markers, prioritised actions, learning loop — never a brain/robot. A
visual *system*, not just pages. (`Intelligence_OS_Visual_Metaphor.md`.)

### D-03.15 — Platforms appear as an ecosystem map, never a logo wall
Amazon, Noon, Talabat, Salla, TikTok, WhatsApp, etc. are nodes in a commerce ecosystem Growmerce
reasons across — demonstrating fluency, not borrowing credibility. (`Platform_Ecosystem_Visualization.md`.)

### D-03.16 — The report is an executive decision brief
Decision-first, evidence-backed, confidence-scored, calm, actionable; revenue and profit visually
distinct. Never a dashboard, PDF, generic audit, or spreadsheet. The diagnostic feels like a calm
intelligence reasoning, not a form. (`Report_and_Diagnostic_Visual_Language.md`.)

### D-03.17 — The trust & confidence visual language is a core differentiator
Evidence strength, confidence levels, assumptions, missing data, verification steps, risk,
priority, and human review are first-class visual elements — calm, honest, reasoning-linked. This is
a moat generic AI/agency brands can't easily copy. (`Trust_and_Confidence_Visual_Language.md`.)

### D-03.18 — Mobile-first
The identity is designed for mobile (TikTok speed, small screens, Arabic reading, WhatsApp) first,
then scaled up. Premium and calm must survive on a phone. Speed is part of the identity.
(`Mobile_First_Brand_Experience.md`.)

### D-03.19 — Claude Design is the next step (not another strategy workstream)
`Claude_Design_Brief.md` is the handoff. The recommended next step is **Claude Design visual
exploration**, not further strategic documentation. Prior workstreams (19, 20, 04, 21) are used
heavily as context; positioning is unchanged.
