# Open Questions — Brand Identity

Unresolved questions for the identity. Surfaced deliberately; none block Claude Design from
starting, all should be resolved during or before visual exploration.

---

## Logo & name

1. **Symbol vs. wordmark dominance.** Is the identity wordmark-led (with an optional supporting
   symbol), or does it need a strong standalone symbol for app/avatar contexts? Lean: wordmark-led,
   but the symbol's necessity should be tested early.
2. **The "g" in جرومرس.** ج is the recommended default, but a native typographer should confirm it
   reads most naturally and premium across target Arabic markets (dialect pronunciation varies).
   Worth validating before the wordmark is crafted.
3. **Concept fusion.** Which 2–3 of the conceptual logo directions (signal / commerce flow /
   pattern recognition / OS-core / structured network / decision mark) combine into the strongest,
   non-cliché mark?

## Naming & descriptor

4. **Arabic descriptor lock.** The descriptor (شركة تشغيل نمو المبيعات الأونلاين vs. tighter
   alternatives) needs finalising with a native commerce writer, consistent with the narrative
   vocabulary (21). Which form, and how short?
5. **Descriptor placement.** Where does the descriptor live (lockup vs. contextual subline), and is
   it always shown or only in first-encounter contexts?

## Typography

6. **Arabic typeface selection.** Which Arabic-first typeface(s) deliver premium + Fusha-readable +
   small-size mobile + data-friendly? Custom vs. licensed? This is the highest-leverage type
   decision.
7. **Display + functional split.** Does the two-tier system (premium display + precise functional)
   hold up, or does one well-chosen family suffice? Tied to Q6.
8. **Numerals.** Arabic-Indic vs. Western digits by context (reports, metrics, mobile) — needs a
   clear, consistent rule.

## Colour

9. **Profit-vs-revenue colours.** What two distinct, calm signals best encode the
   profit/revenue distinction without cliché (no cheap green)? A genuinely novel requirement.
10. **Confidence scale.** What calm visual/colour scale communicates Low→Very High confidence
    *without* red-amber-green good/bad connotations? Core differentiator; needs careful design.
11. **Dark mode.** Is there a secondary dark variant (for the report/console), and where does it
    apply, given the "not a dark dashboard" stance?

## Intelligence visual systems

12. **Ecosystem map depth.** How "live/intelligent" should the platform ecosystem map be at launch
    (static brand asset vs. interactive diagnostic surface)? Scope vs. impact trade-off.
13. **Intelligence-OS metaphor literalness.** How literally should the cognition pipeline be
    visualised vs. abstracted into a calmer brand motif? Balance between "shows how we think" and
    "calm/restrained."

## Scope & process

14. **Identity vs. build boundary.** Which deliverables are brand-system definitions vs. actual
    UI component design (which may belong to a later build workstream)? Avoid overlap.
15. **Validation with real operators.** Should directions be tested with target Arabic-first
    operators before locking, and how?
16. **Motion fidelity at handoff.** How much motion needs to be prototyped now vs. principled-only,
    given that signature motions (confidence change, reasoning progress) are differentiators?
