# Backlog — Commerce Intelligence Patterns

Future patterns and future engine work. Items here are candidates, not commitments. A
candidate pattern is promoted into a library only when it (a) passes the depth/recognition/
action/distinctness/evidence tests and (b) has recurred across ≥2 contexts.

---

## Candidate patterns (need full template + real recurrence)

### Revenue / Profit
- **Subscription Revenue Looks Recurring But Is Actually Re-acquired Every Cycle** — high
  involuntary/passive churn masked by gross MRR.
- **Free Shipping Is A Margin Decision Disguised As A Marketing One.**
- **The 80/20 Has Inverted — Effort Concentrated On The Tail That Produces Least.**
- **Cash Conversion Cycle Is Funding Growth Out Of The Owner's Pocket.**

### Conversion / Journey
- **Mobile Is The Majority Of Traffic And A Minority Of The Design Effort.**
- **Social Proof Exists But Is Generic, Not Relevant To The Specific Buyer Segment.**
- **The Hero Product Is Great And The Second Click Goes Nowhere (Thin Catalogue Depth).**

### Marketplace / Channel
- **Buy-Box / Featured-Offer Loss On The Seller's Own Catalogue.**
- **Advertising Cannibalises Organic Sales The Seller Would Have Won Anyway.**
- **Cross-Border / Multi-Market Listing Inconsistency Splitting Ranking Equity.**

### Pricing / Offer
- **Psychological Price Thresholds Crossed Without Repackaging (e.g. just over a round number).**
- **The Offer Is Priced For The Producer's Cost, Not The Buyer's Reference Value.**
- **Upsell Exists But Fires At The Wrong Moment In The Journey.**

### Retention / Loyalty
- **Win-Back Is Attempted Too Late, After The Customer Has Already Re-solved The Problem.**
- **Loyalty Programme Rewards Behaviour The Customer Would Do Anyway (non-incremental).**

### Operations / Trust
- **Fulfilment Speed Promise And Reality Diverge, Quietly Eroding Reviews.**
- **Customer Service Is A Cost Centre Sitting On The Richest Source Of Pattern Evidence.**
