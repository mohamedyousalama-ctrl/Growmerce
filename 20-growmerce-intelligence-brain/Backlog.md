# Backlog — Growmerce Intelligence Brain

Future capabilities for the intelligence OS. Candidates, not commitments. Items graduate when
they're specified deeply enough to build and have a clear owner.

---

## Cognition engine

- **Pipeline orchestration spec** — turn the conceptual SENSE→LEARN pipeline into an executable
  responsibility map across AI agents (07), orchestration logic, and humans (Open Question #1).
- **Compound-finding selector** — algorithm to surface the 2–3 highest-leverage compound
  findings from a dense pattern graph without overwhelming (Open Question #2).
- **Causal-graph reasoning** — formalise causal chains so the brain reliably identifies the
  intervention head and predicts which downstream patterns a fix will dissolve.
- **Cross-engagement intelligence** — opt-in, privacy-safe reasoning across the client base
  (e.g. emerging competitor/category threats hitting many clients at once) (Open Question #3).

## Evidence & confidence

- **Data-integrity layer** — verify uploaded/connected Tier-3 data is accurate and complete, not
  just present; detect stale, partial, or inconsistent inputs (Open Question #4).
- **Signal-independence detector** — automatically detect shared provenance to prevent
  double-counting in convergence (Open Question #8).
- **Confidence calibration harness** — measure predicted vs. actual to tune bands and movement
  conditions; define minimum sample before track-record weighting is trusted (Open Question #5).
- **Freshness/staleness automation** — per-signal validity windows enforced automatically with
  refresh prompts.

## False-positive control

- **Abstention-rate monitor** — track and target abstention per cognition mode; alert on drift
  toward reckless or useless (Open Question #7).
- **Bias-catalogue expansion** — add field-discovered biases/failure modes; make GUARD's immune
  system itself learn.
- **Adversarial self-test** — a routine where the brain actively tries to break its own top
  findings before surfacing (institutionalising "what would make me wrong?").

## Learning loop

- **Lightweight exhaust capture** — minimal-friction capture of confirmation/outcome/signal/
  language exhausts embedded in delivery (12) so the loop never stalls.
- **Drift/regression monitoring** — flag patterns whose accuracy degrades; auto-route to review.
- **Versioned intelligence registry** — auditable, reversible history of every encoded learning
  update.

## Human-in-the-loop

- **Auto-surface promotion policy** — exact track-record bar to move a pattern from supervised to
  autonomous detection (Open Question #9).
- **Expert calibration tracking** — score expert overrides against outcomes; handle chronic
  miscalibration fairly (Open Question #10).
- **Override-to-training pipeline** — structured flow turning logged overrides into encoded
  improvements.

## Productisation & boundaries

- **Brain-as-SaaS boundary** — define where the brain becomes client-operated vs. internal
  engine; consent model for self-serve data feeding the learning loop (Open Question #11).
- **Cognition-mode transparency** — how clearly to show clients which mode (and confidence
  ceiling) they're in (Open Question #12).
- **Provenance retention policy** — minimum durable provenance for defensibility vs. storage cost
  at scale (Open Question #14).

## Domain expansion

- **Domain-transfer assessment** — test how far the brain's heuristics/patterns hold for
  services, B2B, and subscription-first models; define brain-level adaptations where needed
  (Open Question #13).
