# Decisions — Brand Narrative System

Locked decisions for this workstream. Build on these; do not re-litigate. Changes require a new
decision entry.

---

### D-21.1 — This workstream defines how Growmerce *speaks*, not how it *looks*
The Brand Narrative System governs language — worldview, beliefs, vocabulary, trust-language — across
every surface. It contains no logos, colours, typography, visual identity, design systems, or UI.
That is Brand Identity (03), which will *express* this narrative, not invent it.

### D-21.2 — The narrative is a single operating system, not per-channel copywriting
One worldview, one personality, one vocabulary, one set of guardrails, applied consistently across
website, diagnostics, reports, TikTok, sales, WhatsApp, delivery, and future SaaS. Consistency is
the point; drift is the enemy.

### D-21.3 — The voice is a commercially-scarred operator with the honesty of a scientist
Analytical, evidence-based, practical, operational, direct, calm, quietly confident, intellectually
honest, respectful. Never hyped, salesy, buzzword-heavy, arrogant, vague, academic, cute, robotic,
fear-mongering, or flashy. (`Brand_Personality_Framework.md`.)

### D-21.4 — The narrative is the Intelligence Brain speaking
Trust-language, evidence-awareness, calibrated confidence, and "never fabricate" are bound to the
Brain's Confidence & Trust Model and GUARD (20). The narrative never communicates more confidence
than the Brain computed, and never asserts a finding the evidence doesn't support.

### D-21.5 — Calibrated honesty is a core differentiator, not a hedge
Growmerce states confidence and uncertainty explicitly, names assumptions and data gaps, and says
what would change its mind. "We strongly suspect, here's how we'd confirm" over false certainty.
This is both the ethical choice and the higher-converting one with sceptical operators.
(`Trust_Language_Framework.md`.)

### D-21.6 — Recognition over explanation; patterns over categories
The narrative makes the reader recognise themselves via named patterns (19) in operator language,
rather than describing Growmerce's services. (Pillar 1; aligns with `04-website-ux`.)

### D-21.7 — Revenue and profit are always separated
No Growmerce content celebrates revenue without addressing profit. Reasoning is on fully-loaded
contribution. (Pillar 2; belief 1.) Celebrating revenue alone is a guardrail violation.

### D-21.8 — Six narrative pillars appear everywhere
(1) We find where your sales leak. (2) Revenue is vanity, profit is the truth. (3) Evidence first,
then a conclusion. (4) We tell you how sure we are. (5) Most sales problems are operational, not
magical. (6) We don't advise, we operate. At least one pillar is detectable in any substantial
content. (`Narrative_Pillars.md`.)

### D-21.9 — The vocabulary system is mandatory
Words We Use / Prefer / Avoid / Never Use are binding across all surfaces and all writers (human
and AI). "Never Use" is a hard filter; "Prefer over" is a rewrite rule. (`Vocabulary_System.md`.)

### D-21.10 — The messaging guardrails are bright lines
Never: guarantee outcomes, fake certainty, use hype, claim AI-magic, use manipulative urgency,
make vanity promises, fabricate findings/numbers, hide uncertainty/assumptions/gaps, celebrate
revenue without profit, talk down to the operator, use dark patterns, or sound like an agency/
consultant/SaaS-vendor/hype-startup. (`Messaging_Guardrails.md`.)

### D-21.11 — Narrative adapts by user state, not by changing character
The voice meets the visitor at their state of certainty (Curiosity → Recognition → Diagnosis →
Evidence → Trust → Action → Commitment → Return, from 04), adjusting message and CTA while the
personality stays constant. (`Narrative_by_User_State.md`.)

### D-21.12 — Tone flexes by surface within one character
TikTok is sharper/faster; WhatsApp is warmer; reports are executive; delivery is accountable —
but all stay analytical, evidence-based, calm, and honest. The dial moves between "sharp" and
"warm," never between "honest" and "hype."

### D-21.13 — On TikTok, be bold about the truth, not about the promise
Short-form content stops the scroll with contrarian, true reframes of how commerce works — never
with hype, guarantees, fabricated stats, or growth-hack framing. (`Narrative_for_TikTok.md`.)

### D-21.14 — Selling is by being useful
Sales conversations are working diagnostic sessions, not pitches. No guarantees, no manufactured
urgency, no overclaiming to close. The conversation is the demonstration.
(`Narrative_for_Sales_Conversations.md`.)

### D-21.15 — Delivery communication is honest and accountable
Results are reported with honest attribution, profit separated from revenue, and what-didn't-work
named plainly. Delivery is where the worldview is proven; spin there retroactively discredits the
whole narrative. (`Narrative_for_Service_Delivery.md`.)

### D-21.16 — The standard reaction
Every piece of Growmerce content is judged by one test: does the reader think "these people
understand online sales deeply" rather than "these people are trying to sell me something"? If it
fails, it's rewritten.

### D-21.17 — Positioning is reinforced, never diluted
All narrative reinforces "Online Sales Growth Operations, powered by Commerce Intelligence."
Growmerce is not a marketing agency, content company, dashboard company, generic AI startup, or
software-first company. Prior workstreams (19, 20, 04) are used heavily as context, not rebuilt.
Recommended next: **03-brand-identity** (express this narrative visually).
