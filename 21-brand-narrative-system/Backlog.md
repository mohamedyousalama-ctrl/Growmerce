to produce, train, or encode.

- **Voice persona rule** — resolve institutional "we" vs. first-person operator vs. per-surface mix
  (Open Question #1); document with examples.
- **Founder-voice capture** — codify the founder's natural register into the system so it survives
  a team and AI generation (Open Question #3).
- **Humour/warmth guide** — define the on-brand wit/warmth budget per surface (Open Question #2).

## Trust language

- **Confidence-phrase lexicon** — lock canonical spoken forms for each of the four bands, with
  approved variations to avoid repetition (Open Questions #4, #5).
- **TikTok honesty pattern** — the minimum calibration that fits short-form without overclaiming
  (Open Question #6).
- **Worked trust-language library** — a large bank of before/after rewrites (lazy → Growmerce) for
  training humans and AI.

## Vocabulary & Arabic

- **Bilingual vocabulary lock** — finalise Arabic Use/Prefer/Avoid/Never tiers and the shared
  terminology spine with a native commerce writer (Open Question #7).
- **WhatsApp Arabic register** — decide Fusha-vs-warmer-colloquial line (Open Question #8).
- **Transliteration/style rules** — platform names and commerce terms in Arabic (Open Question #9).

## Content production assets

- **Message bank by pillar** — ready-to-use lines per narrative pillar for each surface.
- **TikTok hook bank** — the belief/rejection lists turned into a library of scroll-stopping,
  honest hooks (one pattern per video).
- **Report language templates** — finding/recommendation phrasings that bake in the trust-language
  grammar.
- **Sales/WhatsApp script patterns** — diagnostic-conversation openers, reframes, and honest-offer
  framings (not rigid scripts).
- **Objection-handling language** — honest responses to "can you guarantee results?", "is this just
  AI?", "why not an agency?" — each consistent with the guardrails.

## Enforcement & tooling

- **Machine-checkable voice rules** — encode banned vocabulary and required confidence-framing as
  automated checks for AI-generated content (Open Question #10).
- **Voice review workflow** — name the voice owner and the lightweight per-surface review gate
  (Open Question #11).
- **Drift detection** — periodic audit to catch the voice sliding toward agency/hype over time
  (Open Question #12).
