# Mobile TikTok Landing Experience

> TikTok traffic is mobile-first, fast, and impatient. A visitor arrives from a 30-second video
> with one pattern in mind and seconds of patience. The mobile landing flow must deliver
> recognition and a next step almost instantly — it is the highest-velocity, lowest-friction
> path in the entire site.

This is where the TikTok Growth Engine (12) hands off to the website, and where the Brain's
fast-judgement mode (20) must perform at its quickest.

---

## 1. The context: who arrives and how

- **Device:** phone, one hand, vertical, in-the-moment.
- **Source:** a specific TikTok video with a specific hook (a single pattern, dramatised).
- **Mindset:** curious, primed by the video, but with seconds of patience and zero tolerance for
  friction.
- **Expectation:** continuity — the landing must instantly match the video's hook, or the
  visitor bounces.

The cardinal rule: **the lander honours the exact promise of the video.** A mismatch between hook
and landing is the fastest way to lose this traffic (this is the *journey-built-for-the-wrong-
channel* pattern from 19 — Growmerce must not commit it on its own site).

---

## 2. The flow (fast, linear, thumb-driven)

```
TikTok hook (one pattern, dramatised)
   ▼
LANDING: the same pattern, named in one line   ← instant continuity + recognition
   ▼
RECOGNITION: "is this you?" + one-line why/how-we'd-know
   ▼
DIAGNOSTIC / TOOL START: one tap, pre-loaded with this pattern
   ▼
QUICK INPUT: paste a link / pick a channel / one chip (Spotlight-style, minimal)
   ▼
PREVIEW INSIGHT: one warm finding + honest confidence
   ▼
CAPTURE: WhatsApp number / "send to WhatsApp" (one tap)
   ▼
REPORT / CONSULTATION CTA: continue the conversation
```

Each step is a single, full-screen, thumb-reachable moment. No step asks for more than one
action. Value (recognition → preview insight) arrives **before** capture, just faster than on
desktop.

---

## 3. Step-by-step principles

| Step | Principle |
|---|---|
| **Landing** | Match the video's hook in the first line; the visitor must feel "yes, this is the thing from the video" within one second |
| **Recognition** | One pattern, operator words, "is this you?" — cold recognition (`Pattern_Recognition_Experience.md`) |
| **Start** | One tap into the diagnostic/tool, pre-loaded with the pattern — no menu, no choice paralysis |
| **Quick input** | The absolute minimum: a pasted link, a tapped channel chip, or skip; the command input does the inference (`Structured_Input_Experience.md`) |
| **Preview insight** | One real, warm finding with honest confidence — the payoff, fast |
| **Capture** | WhatsApp-first, one tap, value already given (`Lead_Capture_Experience.md`) |
| **CTA** | Continue on WhatsApp / get the full report — the warm forward step |

---

## 4. Friction budget (ruthless)

The mobile/TikTok flow operates on a strict friction budget:
- **No** pop-ups, interstitials, cookie walls fighting for the first screen, or autoplaying noise.
- **No** typing required to reach the first insight (tap/paste/skip only).
- **No** forms before value; capture is one field, ideally a WhatsApp tap.
- **No** dead ends; every screen has one obvious forward tap.
- **Minimal** load weight — speed *is* UX here; a slow first screen loses TikTok traffic outright.

Every additional tap or field measurably loses visitors at this velocity. When in doubt, remove a
step.

---

## 5. Continuity with the video (campaign landers)

Each TikTok campaign/video gets a **matched lander**:
- The lander's headline = the video's hook, near-verbatim.
- The pre-loaded pattern = the pattern the video dramatised.
- The example insight = the same scenario the video teased, now made personal.

This 1:1 match means the visitor experiences a single continuous thought from video → site →
recognition, which is what converts impulsive attention into a diagnostic start. (Operationalises
the channel-journey-alignment lesson from 19/20.)

---

## 6. Honesty at speed

Speed must not become hype. Even in the fastest flow:
- The preview insight carries **honest confidence** ("from your link, we strongly suspect…").
- No fabricated numbers; if input is thin, the finding is framed as a possibility to confirm.
- The capture is permissioned and low-pressure.
Fast and honest are not in tension — honesty is what makes the fast conversion *stick* into a real
conversation rather than a bounce after the dopamine fades.

---

## 7. Handling mid-way exits

TikTok visitors leave abruptly. The flow is resilient (full handling in `UX_Edge_Cases.md`):
- Value is delivered **early**, so even a partial visit leaves a recognition impression.
- A one-tap WhatsApp is always present, so an interested-but-leaving visitor can convert in one
  motion.
- No long process that's worthless if abandoned — each step stands alone.

---

## 8. User-State Transition (this surface)

| Lens | Mobile / TikTok Landing |
|---|---|
| **State entering** | 1 Curiosity — primed by a specific video hook, seconds of patience |
| **State leaving** | 2 Recognition → 3 Diagnosis (compressed), ideally toward 7 Commitment via one-tap WhatsApp |
| **Friction** | any friction at all (load, taps, forms) → removed ruthlessly; tap/paste/skip only, no value gated |
| **Trust gained** | instant hook-to-landing continuity + a real warm finding fast |
| **Intelligence revealed** | the single video pattern, then one personalised finding with honest confidence |
| **Next action** | one-tap WhatsApp / full report |

## 9. Landing → Brain mapping (summary)

| Step | Brain (20) | Pattern (19) |
|---|---|---|
| Landing/recognition | fast-judgement + recognition | the video's pattern |
| Quick input | SENSE (minimal) | one signal |
| Preview insight | DECIDE (1 finding) + Confidence | matched pattern |
| Capture → WhatsApp | ACT | route to Growth Ops |

The TikTok lander is the Brain's fast-judgement mode at maximum speed — recognition in a second,
a real insight in a few taps, a warm WhatsApp conversation one tap after that. It turns fleeting
mobile attention into qualified intent without ever feeling like a form or a funnel.
