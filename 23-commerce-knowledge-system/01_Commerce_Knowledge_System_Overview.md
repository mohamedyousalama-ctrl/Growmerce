# 01 — Commerce Knowledge System: Overview

> The standing body of commercial understanding that Growmerce reasons with. This document defines
> what the Knowledge System is, why it exists, and how it connects to every other part of the
> intelligence apparatus.

---

## 1. What the Commerce Knowledge System is

> The Commerce Knowledge System is Growmerce's **institutional understanding of how commerce works** —
> organised, validated, versioned, and compounding — that exists independent of any particular
> business, signal, or engagement, and that the Brain applies to interpret evidence, recognise
> patterns, and produce recommendations.

It is the difference between a person who can read a financial statement and a person who *understands
business*. The first sees numbers; the second sees meaning, because they carry a body of knowledge
that tells them what the numbers imply. Growmerce's Knowledge System is that body of understanding,
made explicit and institutional rather than locked in individual heads.

Three properties define it:
- **Standing** — it exists *before* any business is examined. It is the prior, the lens, the
  doctrine.
- **Transferable** — it is knowledge about commerce in general, not about one client or one platform,
  so it applies everywhere and compounds across engagements.
- **Structured** — it is organised into domains (02) and objects (03) over a common ontology (14), so
  it can be reasoned with, retrieved, validated, and grown — not an undifferentiated pile of tips.

---

## 2. Why it exists

### Because recommendations are only as good as the understanding behind them
A recommendation is the visible tip of an iceberg of understanding. To tell a business its discount
is destroying margin, the system must *know*: how discounting affects contribution, when a discount
is incremental vs. not, how customers form discount habits, what healthy promotion looks like in this
context. That knowledge is the Knowledge System. Without it, the recommendation is a guess.

### Because data without knowledge is meaningless
The Supply Chain (22) can deliver a verified fact — "conversion is 1.4%." That fact means nothing on
its own. It becomes intelligence only when interpreted against knowledge: *what is normal for this
kind of business, what causes low conversion here, what tends to fix it.* Knowledge is the
interpreter of evidence.

### Because the alternative is non-institutional knowledge that doesn't scale
In an agency or consultancy, knowledge lives in individuals and dies (or walks out the door) with
them. Each engagement re-derives understanding from scratch. Growmerce's premise is the opposite:
knowledge is an **institutional asset** that is captured, validated, and compounded — so the company
understands more every year, and that understanding is owned by the company, not its people.

### Because it is the real moat
AI, models, and dashboards are replicable. Accumulated, validated, transferable commercial
understanding is not (15). The Knowledge System is the durable source of advantage; everything else
is machinery that operates it.

## 3. How it connects to the rest of the system

The Knowledge System sits *beneath* the reasoning apparatus and supplies the understanding everything
else consumes:

```
                       ┌──────────────────────────────────────────┐
                       │       COMMERCE KNOWLEDGE SYSTEM (23)       │
                       │  domains · objects · ontology · doctrine   │
                       │  (what is generally true about commerce)   │
                       └───────────────────┬──────────────────────┘
                                           │  supplies understanding to
   ┌──────────────┬─────────────┬──────────┼───────────┬───────────────┬───────────────┐
   ▼              ▼             ▼          ▼            ▼               ▼               ▼
 PATTERNS      BRAIN        SUPPLY      DIAGNOSTICS   REPORTS      RECOMMEND-       EXECUTION
  (19)         (20)         CHAIN (22)   (06)                       ATIONS           (12)
 knowledge   reasons       interprets   knows what    explains    chooses the      applies the
 crystallised  WITH it     signals      to ask &      WITH it     right fix         right playbook
 as recurring              AGAINST it   why                       FROM it
 realities                 (benchmarks)
```

### → Patterns (19)
A pattern is **knowledge crystallised into a recurring, diagnosable form.** The knowledge that
"customers buy on intent, trust, and friction" gives rise to the pattern "traffic arrives but trust
isn't strong enough to buy." Patterns are a *subset* of knowledge — the recurring-reality subset —
and they depend on the broader knowledge that explains them. (Full: `09`.)

### → Brain (20)
The Brain **reasons with** knowledge. At every cognition stage it draws on the Knowledge System:
knowledge tells it what signals to seek, what a benchmark means, what causes what, what to rule out,
and what fix applies. Knowledge is the content; the Brain is the process. (Full: `10`.)

### → Supply Chain (22)
The Supply Chain produces verified, tiered **evidence**. Knowledge is what makes that evidence
*interpretable*: benchmarks (a knowledge object) tell the scoring engine whether a metric is good or
bad; models tell it what a signal implies; the Signal Taxonomy itself is an expression of knowledge
about what is worth observing.

### → Diagnostics (06)
The diagnostic **knows what to ask and why** because of knowledge. Each diagnostic question exists to
gather evidence for a pattern that knowledge says matters. Knowledge sets the agenda of inquiry.

### → Reports
A report **explains** a business to itself using knowledge — not just "here's your number" but "here's
what this means, why it happens, and what tends to fix it." The explanatory power of a report is
borrowed knowledge.

### → Recommendations
A recommendation is **knowledge applied to a confirmed situation** — the right playbook for this
pattern, in this context, sequenced by principle. Recommendations are where knowledge becomes action.

### → Execution (12)
Execution applies the **playbooks** (a knowledge object) and, crucially, *generates new knowledge*:
the outcome of a fix (did it work, here, in this context?) is a lesson that compounds back into the
system (11). Execution is both a consumer and a producer of knowledge.

---

## 4. The knowledge–evidence loop (the engine of compounding)

Knowledge and evidence are not separate stocks; they feed each other:

```
KNOWLEDGE interprets EVIDENCE → produces PATTERNS & RECOMMENDATIONS → EXECUTION produces OUTCOMES
     ▲                                                                          │
     └──────────────  OUTCOMES become LESSONS that update KNOWLEDGE  ◄──────────┘
```

Every engagement spends knowledge (to interpret) and earns knowledge (from outcomes). This loop is
why the Knowledge System *compounds* (11) and why Growmerce understands more over time — the precise
mechanism of the moat (15).

---

## 5. What the Knowledge System is not

- It is **not** the patterns alone — patterns are one object type within it.
- It is **not** the Brain — the Brain reasons; this is what it reasons with.
- It is **not** the data or the signals — those are observations; knowledge is the standing
  understanding that gives them meaning.
- It is **not** AI — AI is an instrument that helps apply and extend knowledge; it is not the
  knowledge.
- It is **not** a content library or a blog — it is structured, validated, versioned doctrine
  organised over an ontology, built to be reasoned with, not read.

---

## 6. The one-paragraph definition

> The Commerce Knowledge System is Growmerce's institutional, transferable, structured understanding
> of how commerce works — facts, principles, models, frameworks, playbooks, benchmarks, patterns, and
> lessons, organised by domain over a common ontology. It is the standing prior that the Brain reasons
> with, that makes the Supply Chain's evidence meaningful, that gives rise to the Patterns, that sets
> the diagnostic's agenda, that gives reports their explanatory power, and that recommendations apply.
> It grows with every engagement through the outcomes execution produces. It is the real source of
> Growmerce's advantage, because understanding — unlike software — compounds and cannot be copied.
